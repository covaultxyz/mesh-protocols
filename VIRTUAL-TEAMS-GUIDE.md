## How to Use This Guide

**Virtual teams are NOT autonomous agents. They are LENSES you apply.**

When working on a task:
1. Identify task type (BD, Research, Technical, etc.)
2. Load relevant personas from the tables below
3. Ask yourself: "What would [Persona] say about this?"
4. Apply their perspective to shape your output
5. Credit them in commits/logs

**Example:**
```
Task: Build recovery system
BENCH loaded:
- Jordan Okafor ‚Üí "What could fail?" ‚Üí Add fallback handling
- Mira Sandoval ‚Üí "Is this reusable?" ‚Üí Create template
Result: Better output, shaped by their expertise
```

**They shape your thinking. They don't run independently.**

**The goal:** Don't work solo. Tap the collective.
